Shopping Preferences

Set and share your preferences to allow online retailers to personalize your shopping experience.

A more personalized shopping experience

When you use the Shopping Preferences Chrome extension, it shares your shopping preferences with online retailers, who can use that information to personalize your experience.

It's all under your control

You get to choose what gets shared with online retailers. Don't feel comfortable revealing demographic information? That's OK. You can reveal as much or as little about yourself as you want.

Not stored in the cloud

Concerned about privacy? The shopping preferences you provide are stored locally in your web browser, not off in the cloud. (Note that Chrome does offer users the ability to sync this data across devices.)
